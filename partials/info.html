Twitch Light Alerts

This tool is supposed to give you "live" Light Alerts for Twitch.

You still need to connect this tool with some third Party application (like Streamlabs or Tipeee Stream) to make a synchronous alert together with your overlays possible.

Future plans are to provide more LED Bulb types (currently only LIFX) and more Alert Providers (if needed).
